Cycle 01: Week 1 of 4.

Standard warm up for the week:

3 min. Jump Rope w/ footwork

Stretch (solo):
At post – Twist out + down – 20 sec./side
At post – Twist in – 20 sec./side
At post – Hips back – 20 sec./side
Stdg. Quad stretch – 20 sec./side
Seated, H2K – 20 sec./side

2 rounds:
10 Jumping Pullups
10 Cobra Push ups
10 Jumping Lunges
10 V-sits
10 1/2 bridges
